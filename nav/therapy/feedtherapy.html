Feeding Therapy

At High Hopes, we offer oral-motor, sensory, and behavioral-based evaluations and therapy for children whose congenital/acquired medical issues, or behaviorally based impairments affect feeding.


Our therapists can work with both you and your child, using specialized techniques, to improve feeding abilities.


Schedule Patient

Common Causes


Feeding/Swallowing Difficulties Often Includes:


  • Complex medical conditions related to heart, lung, and gastroesophageal diseases
  • Oral sensory and motor issues
  • Structural abnormalities, such as cleft lip or palate
  • Neuromuscular disorders
  • Prematurity
  • Developmental disabilities
  • Social emotional and environmental factors

Goals of Therapy:

  • Complex medical conditions related to heart, lung, and gastroesophageal diseasesEnhancing the strength and coordination of the lips, tongue, and jaw
  • Eliminating certain food and drink aversions
  • Lessening resistance to feeding

Our Clinicians


Clinicians at High Hopes are trained in:


  • Sequential Oral Sensory (SOS) Approach to Feeding
  • Food Chaining
  • Beckman Oral Motor Approach
  • Pediatric Massage Therapy
  • Myofascial Release (MFR)
  • Lactation Counseling
