  • Parent's Night Out, December 9th 4-8PM:
    •

    Join us for a night of holiday fun with our clinic and preschool kiddos! The children will rotate to holiday themed stations, and the night will end with snacks and a movie. Volunteers will man the stations, including games, crafts, cookie decorating, etc.

  • Hats Off to High Hopes at the Ryman, January 29th
    •

    Jonathan Cain, of the band Journey, and Friends will host the 20th Anniversary of Hats Off to High Hopes! You won't want to miss this exciting event celebrating High Hopes! Potential volunteer duties include registration, greeter, usher, and merchandise table. Volunteers will rotate duties so everyone has an opportunity to see part of the show.
