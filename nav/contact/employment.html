Employment Opportunities

Working at High Hopes can be rewarded in many different ways.


Although the children here are the ones being served, oftentimes we are the ones that truly feel blessed at the end of the day! Job opportunities at High Hopes range from positions as classroom teachers to pediatric therapists. With such a diverse, talented staff, we believe that teamwork and collaboration are a large part of the success seen here and know that each individual is an integral part of High Hopes.

If you are interested in being a part of the High Hopes Preschool team, please send your resume to Melanie Anderson at manderson@highhopesforkids.org, Director of Educational Services.

If you are interested in being a part of the High Hopes Therapy Clinic team, please send your resume to Kristin Garner at kgarner@highhopesforkids.org, Director of Clinical Services.
