For Volunteers and/or Shadow/Observation Students

High Hopes welcomes volunteers and/or students seeking shadowing or observation hours.

High Hopes is very grateful for all of it's volunteers! We are honored to have individual volunteers and volunteer groups from businesses and schools in the community to help with everything from reading stories in our classrooms, assisting with Easter egg hunts, helping clean and organize in the clinic, and even administrative tasks. We have heard time and time again what a blessing it is for our volunteers to get to be a part of what really happens on a daily basis at High Hopes.

If you are interested in volunteering as an individual or a group, please contact Jessica Mizell at jmizell@highhopesforkids.org or 615-550-1447.

All of our volunteers must complete the following forms prior to volunteering so please click, print, complete and send to Jessica Mizell at jmizell@highhopesforkids.org, fax 615.661.309.8342 or deliver to High Hopes, 301 High Hopes Court, Franklin, TN 37064.

We request that volunteers be at least 16 years of age unless they are accompanied by an adult and that students seeking shadowing or observation hours be committed to at least 20 hours and willing to have a set schedule.
